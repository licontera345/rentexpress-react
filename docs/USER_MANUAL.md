## 1. Logging in and registering

### Log in (customer or employee)

1. Open the application in your browser.
2. Click **Login** in the main navigation.
3. Enter your username in the **Username** field.
4. Enter your password in the **Password** field.
5. If you are an employee, select **Employee** in the role selector; otherwise leave **Customer** selected.
6. Optionally, check **Remember me** to stay signed in on this device.
7. Click **Sign in**.
8. Wait for the application to redirect you to your dashboard. If the credentials are incorrect, an error message will appear; please correct your username or password and try again.

### Register (new customer)

1. Open the application in your browser.
2. Click **Register** (or the equivalent link on the Login page).
3. Fill in all required fields: username, email, password, confirm password, first name, last names, birth date, phone, and address details (street, number, province, city).
4. Read and accept the terms and conditions by checking the corresponding box.
5. Click **Register**.
6. If the form shows errors, correct the highlighted fields and submit again. Once registration succeeds, you will be redirected to the login page. Log in with your new credentials to access your account.

### Recover your password

1. Go to the **Login** page.
2. Click **Forgot password?** (or the equivalent link).
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Click **Send** (or **Submit**).
5. Check your email for a link to reset your password. Open the link, enter your new password as requested, and submit. Then return to the application and log in with your new password.

## 2. Managing your profile (customers)

### View your profile

1. Log in as a customer.
2. Click **Profile** (or **My profile**) in the navigation or dashboard.
3. Review your personal and address information on the profile page.

### Update your profile

1. Navigate to **Profile** as described above.
2. Click **Edit** (or the edit button next to the section you wish to change).
3. Change the desired fields (e.g. name, phone, address). Ensure that all required fields are filled correctly.
4. Click **Save** (or **Submit**).
5. Confirm that a success message appears and that the displayed data has been updated. If an error appears, correct the indicated fields and save again.

## 3. Reservations (customers)

### View your reservations

1. Log in as a customer.
2. Click **My Reservations** in the navigation or dashboard.
3. Review the list of your reservations. Use the filters or pagination if available to find a specific reservation.
4. Click a reservation to see more details if the application offers a detail view.

### Create a new reservation

1. Log in as a customer.
2. Navigate to **My Reservations** and click **New reservation**, or go to **Catalog**, choose a vehicle, and follow the option to create a reservation.
3. Select the **pick-up location** (headquarters) from the list.
4. Select the **return location** (headquarters).
5. Enter the **start date and time** for the rental.
6. Enter the **end date and time** for the rental.
7. If you started from the catalog, ensure the correct vehicle is selected; otherwise search and select the vehicle you want.
8. Review the estimated total and any summary information shown.
9. Click **Submit** (or **Create reservation**).
10. Wait for the confirmation message. You can then view the new reservation in **My Reservations**.

### Cancel or modify a reservation

1. Go to **My Reservations** and locate the reservation.
2. Click the reservation to open its details or actions.
3. If **Cancel** or **Edit** is available, click it and follow the on-screen instructions. Confirm your action when prompted. Note that not all reservations can be cancelled or edited depending on status and policy.

## 5. Managing clients (employees)

### View the list of clients

1. Log in as an employee.
2. Click **Clients** (or **Client list**) in the navigation or dashboard.
3. Use the filters (e.g. name, email, status) if available, then click **Apply** or **Search** to narrow the list. Use pagination to move through pages.

### Create a new client

1. Navigate to **Clients** as above.
2. Click **Add client** (or the plus/add button).
3. Fill in the required fields in the form (e.g. username, email, password, name, address, phone).
4. Click **Save** or **Create**. Confirm that the new client appears in the list.

### Edit a client

1. Go to **Clients** and locate the client in the list.
2. Click **Edit** (or the pencil icon) for that client.
3. Update the desired fields in the form.
4. Click **Save** or **Update**. Confirm that the changes are reflected in the list.

### Deactivate or delete a client

1. Go to **Clients** and locate the client.
2. Click **Edit** (or open the client details).
3. Use the **Deactivate** or **Delete** option if available, and confirm when prompted. Note that deactivation or deletion may be restricted by policy.

## 7. Managing vehicles (employees)

### View the list of vehicles

1. Log in as an employee.
2. Click **Vehicles** (or **Vehicle list**) in the navigation or dashboard.
3. Use the filter panel to filter by status, category, or other criteria, then click **Apply**. Use pagination to browse the list.

### Create a new vehicle

1. Navigate to **Vehicles**.
2. Click **Add vehicle** (or the plus icon in the header).
3. Fill in the required fields (e.g. licence plate, category, status, headquarters, daily price).
4. Upload images if the form allows it.
5. Click **Save** or **Create**. Confirm that the vehicle appears in the list.

### Edit a vehicle

1. Go to **Vehicles** and locate the vehicle.
2. Click **Edit** (or the pencil icon) for that vehicle.
3. Update the desired fields and/or images.
4. Click **Save** or **Update**. Confirm that the list reflects the changes.

### View vehicle details

1. From **Vehicles**, click the vehicle row or a **View** / **Details** link.
2. Review the information in the detail view or modal. Close the view when finished.

### Send a vehicle to maintenance (maintenance inbox)

1. Go to **Vehicles** and open the **Maintenance inbox** (or equivalent) if the vehicle is ready to be marked for maintenance.
2. Follow the on-screen steps to move the vehicle to or from maintenance status. Confirm any dialogs as requested.

### Delete a vehicle

1. Go to **Vehicles**, locate the vehicle, and open **Edit** or the action menu.
2. Select **Delete** and confirm. Note that deletion may be restricted if the vehicle has active reservations or rentals.

## 8. Managing reservations (employees)

### View the list of reservations

1. Log in as an employee.
2. Click **Reservations** in the navigation or dashboard.
3. Use filters (e.g. dates, client, vehicle, status) and click **Apply** or **Search**. Use pagination to browse results.

### Create a reservation (on behalf of a client)

1. Navigate to **Reservations**.
2. Click **New reservation** (or the add button) if available.
3. Select the client, vehicle, pick-up and return locations, and start and end dates.
4. Review the estimate and any summary. Click **Save** or **Create**. Confirm that the reservation appears in the list.

### Edit a reservation

1. Go to **Reservations** and locate the reservation.
2. Click **Edit** for that reservation.
3. Change the allowed fields (dates, vehicle, locations, etc.).
4. Click **Save** or **Update**. Confirm that the list shows the updated reservation.

### Cancel or delete a reservation

1. Go to **Reservations**, locate the reservation, and open **Edit** or the action menu.
2. Select **Cancel** or **Delete** and confirm when prompted. Ensure that the status and business rules allow this action.

### Generate a pickup code

1. Open the reservation details or the list row actions.
2. Click **Generate pickup code** (or equivalent). Note or copy the code and share it with the client as per your procedures.

## 9. Managing rentals (employees)

### View the list of rentals

1. Log in as an employee.
2. Click **Rentals** in the navigation or dashboard.
3. Use filters and pagination to find the desired rental.

### Create a rental from a reservation

1. Navigate to **Rentals** (or locate the reservation in **Reservations**).
2. For a reservation that is ready for pickup, click **Start rental** or **Create rental from reservation** (or the equivalent action).
3. Confirm the data (vehicle, client, dates) and submit. The new rental should appear in the list.

### Complete a rental (return)

1. Go to **Rentals** and locate the active rental.
2. Click **Return** or **Complete rental** for that rental.
3. Fill in any required return information (e.g. condition, mileage) and confirm. The rental status should update accordingly.

### Edit a rental

1. Go to **Rentals**, locate the rental, and click **Edit**.
2. Update the allowed fields and click **Save** or **Update**. Confirm that the list reflects the changes.

## 10. Troubleshooting

- **I cannot log in.** Please verify your username and password. If you are an employee, ensure you have selected the **Employee** role. If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot password?** link and follow the instructions sent to your email.
- **The page does not load or shows an error.** Refresh the page. If the problem continues, check your internet connection and try again later. If the application shows a specific error message, note it and contact support.
- **My session ended unexpectedly.** For security, the application logs you out after a period of inactivity or if your session expires. Please log in again.
- **I do not see a menu option.** Some options are available only to certain roles (e.g. employee-only sections). Ensure you are logged in with the correct account type. If you believe you should have access, contact your administrator.
- **A form will not submit.** Ensure that all required fields are filled and that any validation messages are resolved. Check that dates and numbers are in the correct format. If the problem persists, try again in a few moments or contact support.
